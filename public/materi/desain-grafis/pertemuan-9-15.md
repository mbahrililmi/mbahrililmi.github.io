## PENGANTAR MATERI


Advertising design adalah seni dan ilmu menciptakan komunikasi visual yang
persuasif untuk mempromosikan produk, layanan, atau ide. Berbeda dengan
desain lainnya, advertising design memiliki tujuan spesifik: MEMPENGARUHI
PERILAKU audience (awareness, interest, desire, action).

Good advertising design adalah perpaduan antara:
- VISUAL yang eye-catching dan memorable
- MESSAGE yang clear dan compelling
- STRATEGY yang sesuai dengan target audience
- PSYCHOLOGY yang mempengaruhi decision making

Dalam era digital marketing, desain iklan harus beradaptasi dengan berbagai
platform - dari billboard tradisional hingga Instagram ads, dari poster
hingga video ads. Namun prinsip fundamentalnya tetap sama: capture attention,
communicate benefit, prompt action.

Pada pertemuan ini, kita akan mempelajari teori advertising, prinsip-prinsip
desain iklan, dan praktik membuat advertising design untuk berbagai media.

## MATERI


### A. KONSEP ADVERTISING DAN MARKETING


#### 1. Definisi Advertising

      - Paid communication
      - Identified sponsor
      - Persuasive messaging
      - Via mass media
      - Goal: Influence behavior

#### 2. Fungsi Advertising

      - Brand Awareness: Kenalkan brand/product
      - Information: Edukasi konsumen
      - Persuasion: Convince untuk beli
      - Reminder: Keep brand top of mind
      - Reinforcement: Strengthen brand loyalty

#### 3. Marketing Funnel (AIDA Model)

      - Awareness: Tahu produk exists
      - Interest: Tertarik untuk tahu lebih
      - Desire: Ingin memiliki
      - Action: Beli/daftar/contact

      Design berbeda untuk setiap stage!

#### 4. Types of Advertising

**a. Product Advertising**

         - Promote specific product
         - Focus on features/benefits

**b. Brand Advertising**

         - Build brand image
         - Emotional connection
         - Long-term investment

**c. Institutional Advertising**

         - Corporate identity
         - Company values

**d. Advocacy Advertising**

         - Promote cause/idea
         - Social/political issues

### B. JENIS MEDIA IKLAN


#### 1. PRINT ADVERTISING


**a. Newspaper Ads**

         - Black & white atau color
         - Various sizes (full page, half, quarter)
         - Short lifespan
         - Local reach

**b. Magazine Ads**

         - High quality print
         - Full color
         - Niche targeting
         - Longer lifespan

**c. Brochure/Flyer**

         - Direct distribution
         - Detailed information
         - Portable

**d. Poster**

         - Large format
         - Public spaces
         - Visual impact

**e. Billboard**

         - Outdoor advertising
         - Very large scale
         - Brief message (7 words rule)
         - Viewed from distance/motion

#### 2. DIGITAL ADVERTISING


**a. Social Media Ads**

         - Facebook/Instagram: Square (1:1), vertical (4:5), stories (9:16)
         - Twitter: Landscape (16:9)
         - LinkedIn: Professional context
         - TikTok: Vertical video (9:16)

**b. Display Ads (Banner Ads)**

         - Website placements
         - Standard sizes:
           * Leaderboard: 728x90
           * Medium Rectangle: 300x250
           * Wide Skyscraper: 160x600
           * Mobile Banner: 320x50

**c. Video Ads**

         - YouTube pre-roll
         - Social media video
         - TV commercials
         - Short-form vs long-form

**d. Email Marketing**

         - Newsletter ads
         - Promotional emails
         - HTML design

**e. Search Engine Ads**

         - Text-based
         - Google Ads
         - Bing Ads

#### 3. OUT-OF-HOME (OOH) ADVERTISING

      - Billboards
      - Transit ads (bus, MRT, taxi)
      - Street furniture (bus stops, kiosks)
      - Digital screens/LED boards
      - Guerrilla advertising (unconventional)

### C. PRINSIP ADVERTISING DESIGN


#### 1. Attention (Grab It!)

      - Bold visuals
      - Contrasting colors
      - Unexpected elements
      - Large imagery
      - Human faces (eye contact)
      - Motion (for digital/video)
      - Negative space
      - "Pattern interrupt"

#### 2. Clarity (Make It Clear!)

      - Simple message
      - Clear hierarchy
      - Readable typography
      - One main idea
      - Avoid clutter
      - Focus pada benefit, not features

#### 3. Interest (Keep It!)

      - Relevant to audience
      - Address pain points
      - Show benefits
      - Emotional appeal
      - Storytelling
      - Relatable scenarios

#### 4. Desire (Create It!)

      - Show transformation
      - Testimonials/social proof
      - Limited offer/urgency
      - Aspirational imagery
      - Quality photography
      - Before/after

#### 5. Action (Prompt It!)

      - Clear Call-to-Action (CTA)
      - Easy to understand next step
      - Visible contact info
      - Create urgency
      - Remove friction

#### 6. Brand Consistency

      - Logo visibility
      - Brand colors
      - Brand voice
      - Consistent style
      - Recognition across campaigns

### D. ELEMEN DESAIN IKLAN


#### 1. HEADLINE

      - Most important text
      - Largest size
      - Capture attention
      - Communicate main benefit
      - Types:
        * Direct: "Save 50% Today"
        * Indirect/Question: "Tired of Back Pain?"
        * News: "Introducing the New..."
        * How-to: "How to Lose Weight Fast"
        * Command: "Join Now"
      - Tips:
        * Short dan punchy (5-10 words)
        * Benefit-driven
        * Specific numbers (50% better than "big discount")
        * Power words (Free, New, Now, Proven, etc.)

#### 2. VISUAL/IMAGE

      - Hero image atau illustration
      - Product shot
      - Lifestyle imagery
      - Quality photography/graphics
      - Should support headline message
      - Emotional trigger
      - Dominant element (often 60-70% of ad)
      - Tips:
        * High resolution
        * Relevant to message
        * Shows benefit atau use case
        * Professional quality
        * Human element (relatable)

#### 3. BODY COPY

      - Supporting text
      - Elaborate pada headline
      - Details, features, benefits
      - Credibility (stats, testimonials)
      - Should be scannable
      - Short paragraphs
      - Bullet points
      - Not always necessary (especially outdoor)

#### 4. CALL-TO-ACTION (CTA)

      - Tell audience what to do next
      - Button atau prominent text
      - Action verbs:
        * "Shop Now"
        * "Learn More"
        * "Sign Up Free"
        * "Get Your Discount"
        * "Download App"
        * "Call Today"
      - Visual prominence (color, size, position)
      - Create urgency: "Limited Time", "Today Only"

#### 5. LOGO

      - Brand identifier
      - Usually bottom right atau corner
      - Visible but not overwhelming
      - Consistent placement for campaigns

#### 6. CONTACT INFO

      - Website URL
      - Phone number
      - Social media handles
      - QR code (untuk easy access)
      - Only include relevant channels

### E. LAYOUT UNTUK ADVERTISING


#### 1. Standard Ad Layouts


**a. Mondrian Layout**

         - Grid-based
         - Asymmetric sections
         - Modern dan clean

**b. Circus Layout**

         - Chaotic, many elements
         - Attention-grabbing
         - Common untuk sale ads

**c. Multi-Panel Layout**

         - 2-3 or more panels
         - Tell story or show progression
         - Before/after comparisons

**d. Silhouette Layout**

         - Product on simple background
         - Focus on product form
         - Elegant

**e. Type-Driven Layout**

         - Typography sebagai main visual
         - Bold, creative typography
         - Minimal imagery

**f. Picture Window Layout**

         - Large dominant image
         - Small headline and CTA
         - Visual storytelling

#### 2. Composition Rules

      - F-Pattern (reading pattern)
      - Z-Pattern (eye flow)
      - Rule of Thirds
      - Visual hierarchy: Headline > Image > Body > CTA
      - White space untuk breathing room
      - Alignment dan grid

#### 3. Layout by Medium

      - Print: More text acceptable, detailed
      - Billboard: Maximum 7-8 words, huge visuals
      - Social: Square atau vertical, mobile-first
      - Video: Opening 3 seconds crucial

### F. TYPOGRAPHY UNTUK ADVERTISING


#### 1. Headline Typography

      - Bold, eye-catching fonts
      - High contrast
      - Large size (relative to ad)
      - Sans-serif untuk modern
      - Serif untuk traditional/luxury
      - Custom fonts untuk uniqueness
      - Kerning penting untuk readability

#### 2. Body Copy Typography

      - Readable fonts
      - Appropriate size (not too small)
      - Good line height (1.4-1.6)
      - Contrast dengan background
      - Left-aligned untuk paragraphs

#### 3. CTA Typography

      - Stand out dari rest
      - Bold atau different color
      - Button style dengan padding
      - All caps acceptable untuk CTA

#### 4. Font Pairing

      - Headline + body font different
      - Maximum 2-3 fonts
      - Contrast tapi harmonious

### G. COLOR PSYCHOLOGY DALAM ADVERTISING


#### 1. Color Meanings (Review + Advertising Context)


      - RED: Urgency, excitement, passion, appetite
        * Use: Sales, food, energy drinks, clearance

      - BLUE: Trust, calm, professional, reliable
        * Use: Finance, healthcare, tech, corporate

      - GREEN: Health, nature, growth, eco-friendly
        * Use: Organic products, sustainability, finance (money)

      - YELLOW: Optimism, youthful, attention
        * Use: Children's products, warnings, clearance

      - ORANGE: Friendly, confident, cheerful
        * Use: Calls-to-action, kids, food, fun brands

      - PURPLE: Luxury, creativity, royalty
        * Use: Beauty, luxury goods, creative services

      - BLACK: Luxury, sophistication, power
        * Use: High-end products, fashion, premium

      - WHITE: Purity, simplicity, clean
        * Use: Healthcare, minimalist brands, tech

#### 2. Color Contrast

      - High contrast untuk readability
      - Complementary colors untuk vibrancy
      - Monochrome untuk sophistication

#### 3. CTA Color

      - Should contrast with rest of ad
      - Orange/red untuk urgency
      - Green untuk "go" action
      - Test different colors (A/B test untuk digital)

### H. COPYWRITING UNTUK DESAINER


#### 1. Headline Writing

      - Focus on benefit, not feature
      - BAD: "Our phone has 12 cameras"
      - GOOD: "Capture Pro-Quality Photos Every Time"

      - Use numbers: "3X Faster", "50% Off"
      - Ask questions: "Ready to Transform Your Life?"
      - Make promises: "Guaranteed Results"
      - Create curiosity: "The Secret to..."

#### 2. Body Copy

      - Conversational tone
      - Short sentences
      - Active voice
      - Focus on "you" (audience), not "we"
      - Paint picture of benefit
      - Address objections
      - Proof points (stats, testimonials)

#### 3. CTA Copy

      - Action verbs (Get, Start, Discover, Join)
      - Create urgency (Now, Today, Limited)
      - Remove risk (Free Trial, Money Back Guarantee)
      - Specific (not just "Click Here")

#### 4. Collaboration dengan Copywriter

      - Design supports copy
      - Copy supports design
      - Hierarchy aligned dengan message
      - Visual metaphors for copy concepts

### I. TARGET AUDIENCE DAN STRATEGY


#### 1. Know Your Audience

      - Demographics (age, gender, location, income)
      - Psychographics (interests, values, lifestyle)
      - Pain points dan needs
      - Media consumption habits
      - Decision-making factors

#### 2. Adjust Design untuk Audience

      - Young audience: Bold, trendy, digital-first
      - Older audience: Clear, readable, traditional
      - Luxury audience: Sophisticated, minimal, quality
      - Budget audience: Value, discounts, practical

#### 3. A/B Testing (Digital Advertising)

      - Test different headlines
      - Test different visuals
      - Test CTA colors/text
      - Test layouts
      - Data-driven decisions

### J. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS


#### 1. Print Advertising

      - Resolution: 300 dpi minimum
      - Color mode: CMYK
      - Bleed: 3-5mm
      - Safe area: 5mm from trim
      - File format: PDF (PDF/X-1a for professional print)
      - Fonts: Outlined atau embedded

#### 2. Digital Advertising

      - Resolution: 72 dpi (screen)
      - Color mode: RGB
      - File format: JPG, PNG, GIF (animation), HTML5
      - Size limits: Biasanya max 150kb per ad
      - Dimensions: Follow platform specs exactly
      - Animation: Max 30 seconds, subtle

#### 3. Outdoor/Billboard

      - Resolution: 100-150 dpi (viewed from far)
      - LARGE files (high resolution at large size)
      - CMYK for print
      - Vector elements jika possible
      - Test visibility from distance

## RINGKASAN


Pada pertemuan kesembilan ini, kita telah mempelajari:

1. Konsep Advertising: Paid, persuasive communication dengan goal mempengaruhi
   behavior melalui AIDA model (Awareness, Interest, Desire, Action).

2. Jenis Media Iklan: Print (newspaper, magazine, poster, billboard), Digital
   (social media, display ads, video, email), dan Out-of-Home advertising -
   masing-masing dengan characteristics dan requirements berbeda.

3. Prinsip Advertising Design: Attention, Clarity, Interest, Desire, Action,
   dan Brand Consistency - fundamentals untuk effective ads.

4. Elemen Desain Iklan: Headline (capture attention), Visual (emotional impact),
   Body Copy (details), Call-to-Action (prompt action), Logo (brand), dan
   Contact Info.

5. Layout: Berbagai layout styles (Mondrian, Circus, Multi-Panel, dll) dan
   composition rules untuk different media types.

6. Typography: Headline fonts (bold, eye-catching), body copy (readable),
   dan CTA typography (standout) dengan proper pairing.

7. Color Psychology: Bagaimana warna mempengaruhi emotions dan decisions -
   red untuk urgency, blue untuk trust, green untuk health, dll.

8. Copywriting: Benefit-focused headlines, conversational body copy, dan
   action-driven CTAs yang collaborate dengan visual design.

9. Target Audience: Understand audience demographics dan psychographics untuk
   create relevant, effective advertising design.

10. Technical Specifications: Print (300 dpi, CMYK, bleed) vs Digital (72 dpi,
    RGB, size limits) vs Outdoor (100-150 dpi, large format).

Advertising design yang efektif adalah perpaduan antara creative execution
dan strategic thinking. Bukan hanya tentang "indah", tetapi tentang "bekerja"
- achieving communication dan business goals.

TIPS SUKSES:

Research:
- Study competitors' advertising
- Analyze what works dalam niche yang sama
- Understand target audience deeply
- Look for unique angle

- Start dengan concept/strategy, not tools

- Establish color palette dan fonts early
- Maintain consistency throughout
- Test readability (especially billboard)
- Get feedback from target audience jika possible

Execution:
- Use high-quality images (Unsplash, Pexels untuk stock)

- Feature-focused instead of benefit-focused
- Inconsistency across campaign

- Poor readability

## MATERI


### A. KONSEP 3D GRAPHICS


#### 1. 3D vs 2D

      - 2D: X dan Y axis (width, height)
      - 3D: X, Y, dan Z axis (width, height, depth)
      - 3D objects memiliki volume dan dimension

#### 2. 3D Workflow Pipeline

      - Modeling: Create 3D shapes
      - Texturing: Add colors/patterns
      - Lighting: Setup lights untuk scene
      - Camera: Setup viewpoint
      - Rendering: Generate final image/video

#### 3. Terminology Penting

      - Mesh: 3D object terdiri dari vertices, edges, faces
      - Vertices (singular: Vertex): Points dalam 3D space
      - Edges: Lines connecting vertices
      - Faces: Surfaces defined by 3+ edges
      - Polygon: Face dengan 3+ sides (tri, quad, n-gon)
      - Normals: Direction faces are pointing
      - UV Mapping: 2D representation dari 3D surface untuk texturing

#### 4. Coordinate System

      - X axis: Left-right (Red)
      - Y axis: Forward-back (Green)
      - Z axis: Up-down (Blue)
      - Origin: Center point (0,0,0)

### E. BASIC OPERATIONS


#### 1. Adding Objects (Shift + A)

      - Mesh > Cube, Sphere, Cylinder, Cone, Torus, Plane, dll
      - Curve: Bezier, Circle, Path
      - Text: 3D text object
      - Camera: Add camera
      - Light: Point, Sun, Spot, Area
      - Empty: Helper object
      - Object appears at 3D cursor location

#### 2. Transformations (G, R, S)


**a. Move/Grab (G)**

         - G: Free move (follow mouse)
         - G + X/Y/Z: Constrain to axis
         - G + Shift + X/Y/Z: Move on plane (exclude axis)
         - Ketik number untuk precise value
         - Click atau Enter to confirm
         - Right-click atau Esc to cancel

**b. Rotate (R)**

         - R: Free rotate
         - R + X/Y/Z: Rotate around axis
         - Ketik degrees untuk precise

**c. Scale (S)**

         - S: Uniform scale
         - S + X/Y/Z: Scale on axis
         - S + Shift + Z: Scale on XY plane only
         - Ketik number (0.5 = half, 2 = double)

#### 3. Duplicate Objects

      - Shift + D: Duplicate
      - Alt + D: Linked duplicate (shares data)

#### 4. Delete

      - X: Delete menu
      - Delete: Quick delete

#### 5. 3D Cursor

      - Red-white circle di viewport
      - New objects appear here
      - Shift + Right-click: Place cursor
      - Shift + S: Snap menu
        * Cursor to World Origin
        * Cursor to Selected
        * Selection to Cursor
        * Selection to Grid

### F. MESH EDITING BASICS


#### 1. Enter Edit Mode (Tab)

      - Orange highlighting = edit mode
      - Blue highlighting = object mode

#### 2. Selection Modes (1, 2, 3 keys)

      - 1: Vertex select mode
      - 2: Edge select mode
      - 3: Face select mode
      - Can enable multiple (Shift + 1/2/3)

#### 3. Basic Mesh Operations


**a. Extrude (E)**

         - Creates new geometry extending from selection
         - Most common modeling operation
         - E then G to move
         - E + Z to extrude along Z, dll
         - Fundamental untuk modeling

**b. Inset Faces (I)**

         - Creates inset within face
         - Drag untuk control amount

**c. Bevel (Ctrl + B)**

         - Rounds edges/corners
         - Scroll wheel: Add segments (smoother)
         - Aesthetic dan functional

**d. Loop Cut (Ctrl + R)**

         - Add edge loop
         - Scroll untuk multiple loops
         - Click to confirm position

**e. Subdivide**

         - Right-click > Subdivide
         - Adds more geometry
         - More detail capability

**f. Merge Vertices (M)**

         - Combine selected vertices
         - "At Last", "At Center", dll

**g. Delete/Dissolve**

         - X: Delete menu
         - Vertices, Edges, Faces
         - Dissolve: Remove without creating holes

#### 4. Proportional Editing (O)

      - Toggle on/off
      - Edit with falloff (affects nearby geometry)
      - Scroll: Change falloff radius
      - Good untuk organic shapes

### G. MODIFIERS (NON-DESTRUCTIVE EDITING)


#### 1. Apa itu Modifiers?

      - Operations applied to object
      - Non-destructive (can adjust anytime)
      - Stack modifiers (apply multiple)
      - Only become permanent when "applied"

#### 2. Common Modifiers


**a. Subdivision Surface (Most Important!)**

         - Smooths mesh by subdividing
         - Levels control: Viewport vs Render
         - Creates smooth organic shapes
         - Essential untuk character modeling

**b. Mirror**

         - Mirrors geometry across axis
         - Perfect symmetry
         - Edit one side, other mirrors automatically
         - Great untuk symmetrical objects (characters, vehicles)

**c. Array**

         - Duplicates object in pattern
         - Linear array (count, offset)
         - Radial array (with Empty)
         - Good untuk repetitive elements

**d. Solidify**

         - Adds thickness to surfaces
         - Turn plane into solid object
         - Control thickness amount

**e. Bevel (Modifier)**

         - Bevels edges automatically
         - Non-destructive (vs manual bevel)

**f. Boolean**

         - Union, Difference, Intersect operations
         - Combine multiple objects
         - Cut one object from another

#### 3. Working with Modifiers

      - Add: Properties > Modifier > Add Modifier
      - Stack order matters (top to bottom)
      - Eye icon: Show/hide in viewport
      - Camera icon: Enable/disable for render
      - Apply: Make permanent (can't undo)

### H. MATERIALS DAN SHADING


#### 1. Material Basics

      - Properties Panel > Material Properties (sphere icon)
      - New material: Click "+"
      - Base Color: Object color
      - Metallic: 0=plastic, 1=metal
      - Roughness: 0=glossy/shiny, 1=rough/matte
      - Shading mode: Material Preview (Z > Material Preview)

#### 2. Basic Material Types

      - Plastic/Rubber: Metallic 0, Roughness 0.5
      - Metal: Metallic 1, Roughness 0.2-0.4
      - Glass: Transmission 1, Roughness 0
      - Glossy: Metallic 0, Roughness 0

#### 3. Shader Editor (untuk later/advanced)

      - Node-based material system
      - Very powerful dan flexible
      - Will cover in later sessions

### I. LIGHTING


#### 1. Types of Lights (Shift + A > Light)


**a. Point Light**

         - Emits in all directions
         - Like light bulb
         - For general lighting

**b. Sun Light**

         - Directional, parallel rays
         - Position doesn't matter, only rotation
         - For outdoor/sky lighting

**c. Spot Light**

         - Cone of light
         - Controllable angle dan softness
         - For focused/dramatic lighting

**d. Area Light**

         - Large soft light source
         - Rectangular light surface
         - For soft, even lighting

#### 2. Light Properties

      - Color: Tint of light
      - Power (Watts): Brightness
      - Radius: Size untuk soft shadows

#### 3. Three-Point Lighting (Traditional Setup)

      - Key Light: Main light, brightest
      - Fill Light: Soften shadows, opposite side
      - Rim/Back Light: Separate subject dari background

### J. CAMERA DAN RENDERING


#### 1. Camera Setup

      - Numpad 0: Camera view
      - Select camera: Click di outliner
      - Move camera: G, R, S in object mode
      - Ctrl + Alt + Numpad 0: Align camera to current view
      - Camera properties: Lens, focal length, depth of field

#### 2. Render Engines


**a. Eevee (Default, Recommended untuk Beginners)**

         - Real-time render engine
         - Fast preview
         - Good untuk motion graphics
         - Game-engine style

**b. Cycles (Photorealistic)**

         - Ray-tracing renderer
         - Slower but more realistic
         - Better untuk product viz, architectural

**c. Workbench**

         - Basic preview render

#### 3. Rendering

      - F12: Render current frame
      - View render: Image Editor opens
      - Ctrl + F12: Render animation
      - Save render: Image > Save As
      - Render properties panel: Resolution, output, samples

LATIHAN 1: Navigation dan Interface Exploration

Tujuan: Familiar dengan Blender interface dan navigation

Langkah-langkah:

   - Shift + MMB: Pan view

   - Numpad 0: Camera view
3. Practice selection:

4. Practice transforms:
   - Select cube: G to move, R to rotate, S to scale
   - Try G + X, G + Y, G + Z (axis constraints)
   - Undo dengan Ctrl + Z
5. Experiment dengan view shading (Z key)
6. No need to save - just practice!

LATIHAN 2: Creating Simple Scene with Primitives

Tujuan: Membuat scene sederhana dengan primitive objects

Langkah-langkah:
1. Delete default cube (X)
2. Add primitives (Shift + A > Mesh):
   - Plane (untuk ground)
   - Cube (untuk body)
   - Sphere (untuk head)
   - Cylinders (untuk arms/legs)
3. Arrange untuk membuat simple character atau robot
4. Use G, R, S untuk position dan proportion
5. Try different views (Numpad 1, 3, 7, 0)
6. Save: File > Save As > Latihan02_Primitives_Nama.blend

LATIHAN 3: Basic Mesh Editing

Tujuan: Practice edit mode operations

Langkah-langkah:
1. Start dengan cube
2. Tab: Enter Edit Mode
3. Practice selection modes (1, 2, 3 keys)
4. Extrude (E): Select top face, E, move up
5. Inset (I): Select face, I, drag inward
6. Bevel (Ctrl+B): Select edge, Ctrl+B, drag
7. Loop cut (Ctrl+R): Hover edge, Ctrl+R, scroll untuk multiple
8. Create simple house shape:
   - Cube base
   - Extrude roof
   - Add windows (inset faces)
9. Save: Latihan03_SimpleHouse_Nama.blend

LATIHAN 4: Working with Modifiers

Tujuan: Understand modifier workflow

Langkah-langkah:
1. Create cube
2. Add Subdivision Surface modifier:
   - Properties > Modifier > Add > Subdivision Surface
   - Increase levels: 2 atau 3
   - Observe smoothing
3. Add another cube
4. Add Mirror modifier:
   - Delete half of cube (X > Delete > Faces)
   - Add Mirror modifier
   - Edit one side, observe mirror
5. Create simple character dengan Subdivision + Mirror
6. Experiment dengan other modifiers (Array, Bevel)
7. Save: Latihan04_Modifiers_Nama.blend

LATIHAN 5: Materials dan Lighting

Tujuan: Apply colors dan setup lighting

Langkah-langkah:
1. Create simple scene dengan 2-3 objects
2. Apply materials:
   - Select object
   - Properties > Material Properties
   - New material
   - Change Base Color
   - Adjust Metallic dan Roughness
3. Experiment with different material types
4. Setup lighting:
   - Delete default light
   - Add Key light (Sun atau Area)
   - Add Fill light (softer, opposite side)
5. Switch to Material Preview shading (Z)
6. Adjust lights untuk good looking scene
7. Save: Latihan05_MaterialLight_Nama.blend

LATIHAN 6: First Render

Tujuan: Render final image

Langkah-langkah:
1. Use scene from Latihan 5 atau create new
2. Position camera (Numpad 0, move with G/R)
3. Ensure objects visible in camera view
4. Render settings:
   - Properties > Render Properties
   - Eevee engine
   - Samples: 64 atau higher untuk quality
5. Output settings:
   - Properties > Output Properties
   - Resolution: 1920x1080
   - File format: PNG
6. Render: F12
7. Save image: Image > Save As
8. Save file: Latihan06_FirstRender_Nama.blend

## MATERI - PERTEMUAN 12: ADVANCED MODELING

### A. ADVANCED EDIT MODE OPERATIONS

#### 1. Proportional Editing (O)
- Enable/disable: O key
- Affects surrounding geometry
- Falloff types: Smooth, Sharp, Linear, Constant, Random, Sphere
- Scroll wheel: Adjust influence radius
- Perfect untuk organic shapes dan terrain

**Practice:**
- Create plane, subdivide banyak
- Enable proportional editing
- G + Z dengan vertex selected
- Scroll untuk adjust radius
- Create hills/valleys

#### 2. Knife Tool (K)
- Cut new edges into mesh
- K to activate
- Click untuk define cut points
- Enter to confirm
- Useful untuk add topology where needed

#### 3. Bridge Edge Loops
- Connect two edge loops
- Select two parallel edge loops
- Right-click > Bridge Edge Loops
- Creates connecting geometry
- Great untuk connecting parts

#### 4. Boolean Operations (Edit Mode)
- Intersect (Boolean)
- Union geometry
- Difference untuk cuts
- Intersect untuk overlap only

#### 5. Snap Tool (Shift + S)
- Cursor to Selected
- Cursor to World Origin
- Selection to Cursor
- Selection to Grid
- Cursor to Grid
- Very useful untuk precision

#### 6. Array + Curve Modifier Combination
- Array objects along curve
- Array modifier: Set count
- Curve modifier: Follow curve path
- Great untuk repetitive elements (chain, fence, etc.)

### B. MODIFIERS - ADVANCED USAGE

#### 1. Modifier Stack Order
- Order matters!
- Top to bottom execution
- Example: Mirror > Subdivision > Solidify
- Re-order dengan drag

#### 2. Array Modifier (Advanced)
- Count vs Fixed Count vs Fit Curve
- Offset types: Constant, Relative, Object
- Object offset: Use Empty untuk control
- End caps: Start dan End options
- Combine dengan Curve modifier

#### 3. Curve Modifier
- Deform object along curve
- Must have curve object
- Adjust deform axis
- Perfect untuk hoses, cables, paths

#### 4. Boolean Modifier (Better Workflow)
- Operations: Union, Difference, Intersect
- Use untuk complex cutting
- Fast Boolean add-on (built-in Blender)
- Shift + Ctrl + B: Fast Boolean
- Clean topology after boolean

#### 5. Bevel Modifier (Control Weight)
- Bevel only specific edges
- Edge Bevel Weight dalam Edit Mode
- Limit Method: Weight
- Selective beveling untuk control

#### 6. Shrinkwrap Modifier
- Wrap object onto surface
- Project onto target mesh
- Useful untuk decals, clothing simulation prep

#### 7. Simple Deform
- Twist, Bend, Taper, Stretch
- Angle control
- Use Empty untuk control pivot

#### 8. Displace Modifier
- Displace geometry with texture
- Requires texture (procedural atau image)
- Strength control
- Great untuk surface details

### C. HARD SURFACE MODELING

#### 1. Bevel Workflow
- Sharp edges dengan controlled bevels
- Ctrl + B untuk manual bevel
- Bevel modifier untuk flexibility
- Weight-based beveling

#### 2. Boolean Workflow
- Starting shape
- Boolean operations untuk subtract/add
- Clean up geometry after
- Applies to mechanical parts, architecture

#### 3. Panel Modeling
- Inset faces (I)
- Extrude untuk depth (E)
- Add detail dengan loops (Ctrl + R)
- Tech panels, sci-fi, mechanical

#### 4. Edge Flow
- Clean topology
- Quad-based preferably
- Good edge flow follows form
- Easier to subdivide cleanly

#### 5. Supporting Geometry
- Edge loops near sharp transitions
- Holds form when subdividing
- Prevents unwanted smoothing

### D. ORGANIC MODELING INTRODUCTION

#### 1. Subdivision Surface Workflow
- Start low-poly
- Add edge loops strategically
- Subdivision smooths
- Control form dengan edge placement

#### 2. Sculpting Basics (Optional Advanced)
- Sculpt Mode: Dynamic topology
- Brushes: Draw, Grab, Smooth, Crease
- For organic characters, creatures
- Different workflow dari polygon modeling

#### 3. Retopology Concepts
- Clean mesh over sculpt
- Low-poly from high-poly
- Better topology untuk animation
- (Advanced topic, intro only)

### E. REFERENCE IMAGES

#### 1. Adding Reference Images
- Shift + A > Image > Reference
- Load image dari file
- Position in scene
- Align dengan views (front, side, top)

#### 2. Background Images
- Camera > Background Images
- Add image
- Opacity adjustment
- Helpful untuk exact modeling

#### 3. Modeling dari Reference
- Align reference to ortho views
- Model over reference
- Check proportions frequently
- Professional workflow

## MATERI - PERTEMUAN 13: MATERIALS, TEXTURES, LIGHTING

### A. SHADER EDITOR DAN NODE-BASED MATERIALS

#### 1. Understanding Shader Editor
- Open: Shading workspace
- Node editor at bottom
- Principled BSDF: Main material node
- View materials in Material Preview (Z)

#### 2. Principled BSDF Parameters (Deep Dive)
- Base Color: Diffuse color
- Metallic: 0=dielectric, 1=metal
- Roughness: 0=mirror, 1=rough
- IOR: Index of refraction (glass: 1.45)
- Transmission: Glass/transparency
- Emission: Self-illuminating
- Alpha: Transparency

#### 3. Adding Nodes (Shift + A)
- Texture nodes (Image, Noise, Voronoi, etc.)
- Color nodes (ColorRamp, Mix, Hue/Sat)
- Vector nodes (Mapping, Normal, Bump)
- Converter nodes (Math, ColorRamp)
- Shader nodes (Mix Shader, Add Shader)

#### 4. Connecting Nodes
- Drag from output to input socket
- Color coding: Yellow=color, Green=shader, Purple=vector
- Ctrl + Shift + Click: Preview node
- X to delete connection

### B. PROCEDURAL TEXTURES

#### 1. Noise Texture
- Add detail, variation
- Scale: Detail level
- Detail: Octaves
- Distortion: Warping
- Use: Roughness variation, bump maps

#### 2. Voronoi Texture
- Cell/organic patterns
- Distance metric: Euclidean, Manhattan
- Scale untuk cell size
- Use: Procedural patterns, materials

#### 3. Musgrave Texture
- Fractal noise
- Great untuk natural surfaces
- Terrain, rock textures

#### 4. Wave Texture
- Sine waves, bands
- Rings atau bands direction
- Scale dan distortion
- Use: Wood, water, rings

#### 5. Magic Texture
- Psychedelic patterns
- Turbulent texturing

#### 6. ColorRamp Node
- Remap value ranges to colors
- Essential untuk control
- Adjust color stops
- Interpolation types

#### 7. Combining Textures
- Mix node: Blend textures
- Factor control untuk mixing
- Multiple textures untuk complexity

### C. IMAGE TEXTURES DAN UV MAPPING

#### 1. UV Unwrapping Basics
- UV = 2D coordinates untuk 3D surface
- Every face needs UV coordinates
- UV Editing workspace
- Select edges for seams
- Mark Seam (Ctrl + E > Mark Seam)
- U > Unwrap

#### 2. UV Unwrap Methods
- Unwrap: Standard unwrap
- Smart UV Project: Automatic
- Cube Projection: For box shapes
- Cylinder/Sphere Projection: For those shapes
- Project from View: Current view

#### 3. UV Editor
- See unwrapped UVs
- Edit UV layout
- Scale, rotate, position islands
- Minimize stretching dan distortion

#### 4. Adding Image Texture
- Shader Editor: Add > Image Texture
- Connect to Base Color atau other inputs
- Open image file
- UV coordinates used automatically

#### 5. Bump dan Normal Maps
- Bump Map: Simulate surface detail
- Normal Map: More detail, RGB encoding
- Connect to Normal input via Bump atau Normal Map node
- Strength control

### D. ADVANCED LIGHTING

#### 1. Three-Point Lighting (Review + Detail)
- Key Light: 45° angle, brightest
- Fill Light: Opposite, 50% intensity
- Rim Light: Behind subject, edge lighting
- Ratios matter: 2:1 atau 4:1 key to fill

#### 2. HDRI Lighting
- High Dynamic Range Image
- Environment lighting
- Add: World Properties > Color > Environment Texture
- Load HDR image
- Rotate for direction control
- Free HDRIs: Poly Haven (polyhaven.com)

#### 3. Area Light Techniques
- Large soft light source
- Size = softness
- Multiple area lights untuk control
- Portals untuk indoor lighting

#### 4. Light Linking (Optional Advanced)
- Control which lights affect which objects
- Separate layers
- Complex scenes

#### 5. Volumetric Lighting
- Light shafts, fog
- Add > Volume > Principled Volume
- Density control
- Beautiful atmospheric effects

#### 6. Emissive Materials
- Objects that emit light
- Emission input di Principled BSDF
- Strength control
- Good untuk screens, lights

### E. RENDER SETTINGS OPTIMIZATION

#### 1. Eevee vs Cycles (Deeper)

**Eevee:**
- Real-time, rasterization
- Fast previews
- Good untuk stylized, non-photoreal
- Motion graphics friendly
- Sampling: 64-256

**Cycles:**
- Ray-tracing, path tracing
- Photorealistic
- Slower but more accurate
- Glass, caustics, accurate shadows
- Sampling: 128-4096 depending on complexity

#### 2. Sampling
- More samples = less noise, longer render
- Viewport samples vs Render samples
- Denoising: Enable untuk cleaner results

## MATERI - PERTEMUAN 14: ANIMATION BASICS

### A. ANIMATION FUNDAMENTALS

#### 1. Principles of Animation (12 Principles - Simplified)
- Squash and Stretch: Flexibility
- Anticipation: Prepare for action
- Staging: Clear presentation
- Straight Ahead vs Pose-to-Pose
- Follow Through: Secondary motion
- Slow In and Slow Out: Easing
- Arcs: Natural motion paths
- Secondary Action: Supporting action
- Timing: Speed defines character
- Exaggeration: Dramatic effect
- Solid Drawing: Form and weight
- Appeal: Charisma

(Concepts developed by Disney animators, applicable to 3D)

#### 2. Frame Rate
- 24 fps: Film standard
- 30 fps: TV/video standard
- 60 fps: Smooth video/games
- Blender default: 24 fps
- Output Properties > Frame Rate

#### 3. Timeline
- Bottom panel
- Playback controls
- Current frame indicator (blue line)
- Start/End frame

#### 4. Dope Sheet
- Keyframe editor
- View all keyframes
- Adjust timing
- Clean workflow

### B. KEYFRAME ANIMATION

#### 1. What is Keyframe?
- Saved state di specific frame
- Blender interpolates between keyframes
- Foundation dari animation

#### 2. Setting Keyframes (I)
- Select object
- Pose object (G, R, S)
- I key: Insert Keyframe menu
  * Location
  * Rotation
  * Scale
  * Location & Rotation & Scale
- Alternatively: Right-click property > Insert Keyframe

#### 3. Keyframe Workflow
- Frame 1: Set initial pose (I)
- Move to frame X
- Change pose
- Insert keyframe (I)
- Repeat
- Scrub timeline to playback

#### 4. Auto-Keyframe
- Enable: Timeline > Auto-Keying button (record icon)
- Automatically keys changes
- Faster workflow
- Be careful - keys everything!

#### 5. Playback
- Spacebar: Play/pause
- Shift + Left/Right arrow: Jump to keyframes
- Left/Right arrow: Previous/next frame
- Alt + A: Play backward

### C. GRAPH EDITOR

#### 1. What is Graph Editor?
- Visual representation of animation curves
- F-curves (function curves)
- Control interpolation
- Adjust timing dan easing

#### 2. Interpolation Types
- Linear: Constant speed (mechanical)
- Bezier: Smooth curves (natural)
- Constant: No interpolation (stepped)

#### 3. Handles
- Bezier handles: Control curve shape
- V: Handle type menu
  * Auto
  * Vector
  * Aligned
  * Free

#### 4. Easing
- Ease In: Slow start
- Ease Out: Slow end
- Ease In/Out: Slow both ends
- Adjust handles untuk control

#### 5. Editing F-Curves
- Select keyframes
- G/R/S untuk move/rotate/scale
- Additive editing
- Smooth keyframes: Alt + O

### D. ANIMATING DIFFERENT PROPERTIES

#### 1. Location Animation (Movement)
- Object moves dalam space
- X, Y, Z independently keyframeable
- Paths dan trajectories

#### 2. Rotation Animation (Spin)
- Object rotates
- Euler angles vs Quaternions
- Euler: Gimbal lock issues (advanced topic)
- Quaternion: Smoother, no gimbal lock

#### 3. Scale Animation (Size Change)
- Grow/shrink
- Per axis scaling
- Logo reveals, transformations

#### 4. Material Property Animation
- Keyframe material parameters
- Example: Fade in (animate Alpha)
- Color changes
- Emission strength (glow effects)
- Right-click parameter > Insert Keyframe

#### 5. Camera Animation
- Animate camera position
- Animate camera rotation
- Animated focus (Depth of Field)
- Reveals, flyovers

#### 6. Modifier Animation
- Some modifiers keyframeable
- Array count, modifier strength
- Dynamic effects

### E. SIMPLE ANIMATION PROJECTS

#### 1. Bouncing Ball
- Classic animation 101
- Location keyframes
- Squash and stretch dengan scale
- Timing: Faster at bottom, slower at top
- Graph editor: Adjust easing

#### 2. Logo Reveal
- Scale from 0 to 1
- Or position from off-screen
- Rotation for dynamism
- Emission glow in

#### 3. Product Turntable
- Object centered
- Camera orbits around (atau rotate object)
- 360° rotation over 60-120 frames
- Smooth rotation (linear interpolation)

#### 4. Text Animation
- Fade in dengan material alpha
- Or scale up
- Position animation
- Letter-by-letter (advanced: use child objects)

#### 5. Camera Fly-Through
- Camera moves through scene
- Multiple keyframes untuk path
- Adjust speed dengan timing
- Architecture walkthroughs

### F. RENDERING ANIMATION

#### 1. Render Settings
- Output Properties
- Output path: Choose folder
- File format: FFmpeg video (MP4)
  * Or PNG sequence (safer, can resume)
- Frame range: Start dan End

#### 2. Rendering Process
- Ctrl + F12: Render animation
- Or Render > Render Animation
- Renders frame by frame
- Progress bar shows completion
- Can take long time!

#### 3. Video Output
- FFmpeg settings:
  * Container: MPEG-4
  * Video Codec: H.264
  * Output Quality: High Quality atau Perceptually Lossless
  * Audio codec: AAC
- Render to video file directly

#### 4. Image Sequence Output
- Format: PNG
- Renders individual frames
- Numbered: 0001.png, 0002.png, etc.
- Safer: Can resume if crashes
- Composite in video editor after

#### 5. Preview Renders
- Lower resolution (50%)
- Lower samples
- Test before final render
- Adjust animation based on preview
