Selamat Datang di Mata Kuliah Rekayasa Video dan Audio!

Dalam era digital saat ini, konten multimedia menjadi bagian integral dari komunikasi, entertainment, pendidikan, dan bisnis. Kemampuan untuk memproduksi audio dan video berkualitas tinggi bukan lagi eksklusif untuk studio profesional dengan budget besar. Berkat perkembangan teknologi dan software open-source, siapa saja dapat belajar dan menghasilkan karya audio-visual profesional.

Yang membedakan mata kuliah ini adalah fokus pada penggunaan software FREE dan OPEN-SOURCE yang powerful dan profesional: Audacity, Ardour, DaVinci Resolve, dan Kdenlive. Dengan menguasai tools ini, Anda akan memiliki skill yang langsung applicable untuk karir di content creation, film production, advertising, broadcasting, game development, dan banyak lagi.

Apa itu Rekayasa Video dan Audio?

Definisi

Rekayasa Video dan Audio adalah disiplin ilmu dan praktik yang melibatkan Rekayasa Audio (Recording, Editing, Mixing, Mastering) dan Rekayasa Video (Pre-production, Production, Post-production, Distribution).

Aplikasi di Berbagai Industri

Entertainment & Media
Film production, TV broadcasting, music industry, radio, podcast production.
Advertising & Marketing
TV commercials, video ads, social media content, corporate videos.
Education & Training
E-learning videos, educational YouTube channels, documentary, webinars.
Gaming & Interactive Media
Game sound design, cutscenes, voice acting, interactive installations.
Journalism & Documentary
News reporting, field recording, documentary filmmaking.
Event Production
Live event coverage, conference recording, wedding videography, live streaming.

Demokratisasi & Creator Economy

YouTube: 500+ jam video diupload setiap menit. Podcast: 5+ juta podcast dengan 70+ juta episode. TikTok: 1+ miliar users. Content creators kini menjadi full-time profession dengan multiple revenue streams (ads, sponsorship, merchandise, courses), dan influencer marketing menjadi industri senilai $15+ miliar secara global.

Evolusi Teknologi Multimedia ▼
Audio: 1970s analog tape → 1980s digital recording (DAT, CD) → 1990s computer-based DAW → 2000s software mainstream → 2010s cloud collaboration → 2020s AI-powered tools, spatial audio.

Video: 1950s-1990s analog video (VHS, Betacam) → 1990s digital video tape (MiniDV) → 2000s tapeless recording → 2010s 4K, mirrorless, smartphone videography → 2020s 8K, HDR, virtual production, AI-enhanced editing.

Software yang Akan Kita Gunakan

Dalam mata kuliah ini, kita akan fokus pada FREE dan OPEN-SOURCE software yang powerful dan digunakan secara profesional di industri.

Audacity (Audio Editor)

Free, open-source audio editor dan recorder. Cross-platform, multi-track recording dan editing, plugin architecture (VST, LV2, Nyquist).

  • Kelebihan: Completely free, simple interface, excellent untuk podcast/voice over/cleanup, light on resources
  • Keterbatasan: Tidak ideal untuk complex music production, limited MIDI, basic mixing
  • Use Cases: Podcast, audio cleanup, simple editing, format conversion, recording lectures
Ardour (Digital Audio Workstation)

Professional-grade DAW, open-source (pay-what-you-want), non-destructive editing, unlimited tracks/busses/plugins.

  • Kelebihan: Professional features, advanced mixing/routing, video sync, excellent for music production
  • Keterbatasan: Steeper learning curve, requires better hardware, smaller community
  • Use Cases: Music production, multi-track recording, film audio post-production
DaVinci Resolve (Video Editor, Color, VFX, Audio Post)

Professional video editing, free version dengan 95% fitur Studio, industry-standard color grading. Integrated editing, color, Fusion (VFX), Fairlight (audio). Digunakan di produksi Hollywood.

  • Kelebihan: Tools professional-grade, integrated workflow dalam satu aplikasi, free version sangat powerful
  • Keterbatasan: Heavy on system resources (butuh GPU baik), learning curve tinggi
  • Use Cases: Professional editing, color grading, VFX/compositing, audio post-production, film/commercial
Kdenlive (Video Editor)

Free, open-source video editor berbasis MLT Framework. Cross-platform, multi-track editing, effects dan transitions.

  • Kelebihan: Completely free, lebih ringan dari DaVinci, good proxy editing workflow, intuitive
  • Keterbatasan: Tidak sepowerful DaVinci Resolve, limited color grading, occasional instability
  • Use Cases: YouTube video, educational videos, social media content, alternatif PC kurang kuat
  • OBS Studio: Screen recording, live streaming, virtual camera
  • GIMP: Image editor untuk thumbnails dan graphics
  • Blender: 3D modeling, animation, rendering, Video Sequence Editor built-in
  • Freesound.org & YouTube Audio Library: Free sound effects dan music, royalty-free

Instalasi dan Setup Software

Spesifikasi Minimum
CPU: Intel i5 gen 8 / Ryzen 5
RAM: 8GB DDR4
GPU: Integrated (UHD 630+)
Storage: 256GB SSD + 50GB free
OS: Windows 10 / macOS 10.15 / Ubuntu 20.04
Spesifikasi Recommended
CPU: Intel i7/i9 / Ryzen 7/9
RAM: 16GB (32GB ideal 4K)
GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1650+ (recommended untuk DaVinci)
Storage: 512GB NVMe + External HDD
OS: Windows 11 / macOS latest / Ubuntu 22.04
Langkah Instalasi ▼
Audacity: Download dari audacityteam.org/download, install default settings, configure recording/playback device, optional install LAME MP3 & FFmpeg.

DaVinci Resolve: Download dari blackmagicdesign.com (versi non-Studio), isi registration form, install (10+ GB), configure Project Settings dan GPU di Preferences.

Kdenlive: Download dari kdenlive.org/download, install via Microsoft Store/DMG/AppImage, configure proxy dan cache location.

Ardour (optional): Download dari ardour.org/download.html, pay-what-you-want, configure audio interface dan MIDI devices.
Konfigurasi Dasar & Storage Organization ▼
Audio Settings: Sample Rate 48000 Hz, Bit Depth 24-bit, Buffer Size 512-1024 samples.

Video Settings (DaVinci): Timeline Resolution 1920x1080, Frame Rate 25/30fps, Playback Half Resolution, enable optimized media.

Folder Structure: My Projects/ → Audio Projects/ (Recordings, Projects, Exports) dan Video Projects/ (Footage, Projects, Exports, Assets: Music/SFX/Graphics).
Troubleshooting Umum ▼
  • Audacity tidak detect microphone: Edit > Preferences > Devices, pilih Recording Device yang benar, restart Audacity
  • DaVinci Resolve playback lag: Set Timeline Proxy Mode ke Half/Quarter Resolution, Generate Optimized Media, tutup aplikasi lain
  • RAM tidak cukup: Tutup aplikasi tidak perlu, gunakan Proxy Editing, render-in-place untuk efek berat
  • GPU issues (DaVinci): Update GPU drivers, cek konfigurasi GPU di Preferences > System

Praktikum Pertemuan 1

Praktikum 1: Software Installation & Verification
  • Install Audacity, test recording dan playback, screenshot interface
  • Install DaVinci Resolve atau Kdenlive, buat project baru, import sample, drag ke timeline, test playback
  • System check: CPU, RAM, GPU, storage space (Task Manager/Activity Monitor)
  • Deliverable: 3 screenshot + laporan singkat 1 paragraf tentang issues & kecukupan spesifikasi
Praktikum 2: Interface Exploration
  • Audacity: identifikasi transport controls, selection/envelope/draw tool, waveform display, track panel, effects menu
  • Generate 3 tone (440Hz, 523Hz, 659Hz) di 3 track berbeda, mainkan bersama (A major chord), export sebagai WAV
  • DaVinci/Kdenlive: identifikasi Media Pool, Timeline, Viewer, Inspector. Import 2-3 clip, trim, tambah transisi dan title, export MP4
  • Deliverable: screenshot berlabel, file audio & video hasil export, catatan personal
Praktikum 3: Basic Recording & Editing
  • Set input device, lingkungan tenang, test level -12dB hingga -6dB peak
  • Rekam script perkenalan diri, trim silence, normalize, noise reduction jika perlu, export MP3
  • Rekam video perkenalan 15-30 detik (smartphone/webcam), import ke video editor, trim, color correction dasar, export MP4 1080p
  • Deliverable: file project Audacity (.aup3), audio MP3, video sumber & hasil edit, refleksi 150 kata

Ringkasan & Persiapan Pertemuan Selanjutnya

Key Takeaways

Mata kuliah terdiri 16 pertemuan (Audio 1-7, Video 9-14, Project 15-16) dengan fokus software FREE & OPEN-SOURCE. Ruang lingkup mencakup Recording, Editing, Mixing, Mastering (audio) dan Cinematography, Editing, Color Grading, VFX (video). Career range Rp 5-30+ juta/bulan tergantung experience dan spesialisasi. Remember: Learning audio dan video production adalah journey, bukan race.

Persiapan Pertemuan 2: Dasar-Dasar Audio Digital
  • Materi: fisika suara (gelombang, frekuensi, amplitudo, phase), akustik (resonansi, reverb, echo), digital audio (sampling rate, bit depth, Nyquist theorem), format audio, dynamic range dan SNR
  • Pastikan Audacity sudah ter-install dan berfungsi baik
  • Siapkan headphone/earphone untuk analisis audio detail
  • Pertanyaan refleksi: Mengapa perlu memahami fisika suara untuk audio engineering? Apa perbedaan analog dan digital audio?
